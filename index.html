The Roboman

Roboman is a name given to humans who were converted by the Daleks into obedient slaves during their invasion of Earth in the 22nd century. The Daleks operated on their brains and fitted them with helmet-like headsets that controlled their actions. The Robomen were armed with machine guns and used for patrols and overseeing other slaves.
